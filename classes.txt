Class: Dog

height (integer)
eyeColor (string)

happyDog (boolean)

Methods:

feedDog (decreases attribute hungryDog value, increases weight attribute value)

makeSit (keeps positionDog constant)
takeOnRun (changes positionDog more quickly that walkDog)
